Lifemate bundles the areas that are usually scattered across five apps: calendar, fitness and nutrition tracking, finances and learning notes — in one interface, with an AI layer on top. Instead of just collecting data, Lifemate helps with planning: training and nutrition plans are generated and adapted to your goals.

Technically, Lifemate is a monorepo with two frontends — a web app for desktop and mobile, and a touch variant for tablets and smart displays — on a shared codebase: Next.js and React on the frontend, Express and Firestore on the backend, OpenAI for the planning features.

Lifemate is my own project at Racep Labs — from architecture to deployment.
