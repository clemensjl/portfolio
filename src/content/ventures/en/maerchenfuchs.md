Märchenfuchs tells children bedtime stories that exist only once: with their own name, their favorite topics, matched to their age. Parents set up once what their child loves — the app turns that into a new story every evening.

There is more behind it than a text generator: stories follow age-appropriate narrative patterns, and parents stay in control of topics and length. The goal is an evening ritual children look forward to.

We are building Märchenfuchs as a team of three under Racep Labs. I own the technical side: the Flutter app, the Supabase backend and the story pipeline behind it.
